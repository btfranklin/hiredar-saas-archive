About Hiredar

What We Do

Hiredar is an AI-powered talent matching platform built for professional recruiters. We ingest your résumé backlog, standardize profiles, and match them to your job descriptions using transparent, multi-lens scoring.

The result is a defendable shortlist with plain-English rationales you can share with hiring managers in minutes. Not a sourcing marketplace. Not here to replace your ATS—just make it pay off.

Our Technology

Built with modern Python/Django and explainable AI for résumé parsing and semantic matching. Privacy-first by design—no keyword gaming or black-box scores you can't defend.

Designed to be fast, reliable, and user-friendly for busy recruiters and hiring teams. US recruiters only, credits-based usage, no subscriptions.
