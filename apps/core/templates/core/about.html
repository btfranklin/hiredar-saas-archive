About Hiredar

What We Do

Hiredar is an AI-powered talent matching platform that connects recruiters with qualified job seekers through intelligent résumé parsing and candidate matching.

We help recruiters quickly identify the best candidates for their roles, while helping job seekers get discovered for opportunities that match their skills and experience.

Our Technology

Built with modern Python and Django, powered by advanced AI for résumé parsing and semantic matching. We use industry-leading practices for data security and privacy protection.

Our platform is designed to be fast, reliable, and user-friendly for both recruiters and job seekers.
