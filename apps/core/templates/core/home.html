Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hiredar really free for job seekers?

Absolutely. Job seekers will never pay to create a profile, receive role recommendations, or message with recruiters on Hiredar. We fund the platform through recruiter credits only.

What counts as a credit for recruiters?

Credits are used for: résumé processing (1 credit), contacting candidates (2 credits), and exporting shortlists (5 credits). Browsing candidates and viewing matches are free.

Do my free or purchased credits ever expire?

No. Credits stay in your account until you decide to use them—no monthly minimums or “use-it-or-lose-it” deadlines.

How does Hiredar generate AI-scored matches?

We combine large-language-model embeddings with domain-specific heuristics to compare résumé content to your job requirements across skills, experience, qualifications, and seniority.

Is my data secure and private?

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. Job seeker profiles are only visible to registered recruiters. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I integrate Hiredar with my existing ATS?

Export matched candidates as CSV or PDF at any time. We are also working on direct integrations—contact us to discuss your specific ATS needs.
