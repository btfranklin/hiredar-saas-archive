Who We Are

Hiredar is an AI-powered hiring platform headquartered in sunny Phoenix, Arizona. We believe great careers begin with transparent, human-centric matches—not keyword filters or ghost job listings.

Our Mission

To eliminate the busy-work of recruiting so people can focus on relationships, not résumés. We turn unstructured data into clear, data-backed insights that surface hidden talent and speed up hiring for everyone involved.

What Makes Us Different

  • No Ghost Jobs: Every role and candidate is real. Period.
  • Explainable AI: Our multi-lens matching engine tells you why a candidate appears, not just their rank.
  • Multiple Candidate Views: See top matches and wildcard candidates to avoid missing hidden gems.
  • Built to Fix a Broken System: Created by a software engineer who saw that recruiters are overwhelmed and great candidates are getting lost in the ocean of applicants. Hiredar is designed to help both sides do better.

How It Started

Hiredar began with a simple belief: that technology and AI can be used compassionately to help people find the right opportunities, and to support recruiters who genuinely care about making great matches. Our goal is to make the hiring process more human, more fair, and more optimistic—for everyone involved.

Looking Ahead

Today we serve professional recruiters who need results, fast. Tomorrow we will open the doors to job seekers and hiring managers alike—with the same commitment to clarity, fairness, and speed.
