Effortless Bulk Uploads (Because Your Time Matters)

Say goodbye to time-consuming, one-by-one résumé uploads. Hiredar handles bulk uploads of PDF and DOCX files seamlessly. No complex drag-and-drop gymnastics, just straightforward efficiency. Focus on finding talent, not wrestling with files.

  • Supports industry-standard PDF and DOCX formats
  • Process hundreds of résumés in a single batch
  • Secure and reliable file handling

Instant AI-Scored Matches (No Unicorns, Just Reality)

Hiredar's intelligent engine doesn't just read résumés; it understands them. It parses text, analyzes skills, experience, and qualifications against your job requirements, and provides an instant, objective score. Uncover top candidates that manual screening might miss, across multiple lenses.

  • Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) for deep understanding
  • Scores skills, experience, and qualifications against role requirements
  • Reduces bias and ensures consistent evaluation

Shortlists That Make Sense (Context, Not Keywords)

Move beyond simple lists. Hiredar helps you build dynamic shortlists with valuable context. Get AI-generated taglines summarizing candidate strengths, access deeper profile insights, and easily manage your selections. One-click additions make it fast and intuitive.

  • Quickly identify key strengths with AI-generated summaries
  • Build and refine your shortlist with simple add/remove controls
  • Drill into structured candidate profiles for deeper insights

Shareable Reports That Convince (Less Back-and-Forth)

Communicating your findings is crucial. Hiredar allows you to generate clean, professional reports of your shortlisted candidates in both PDF and CSV formats. Perfect for sharing with hiring managers, clients, or for your own records.

  • Clean, well-formatted PDF reports for presentations
  • Flexible CSV exports for data analysis or ATS integration
  • Standardized layout ensures every report looks polished and professional
